Theory:


Cooling Bedsheets

Theoretical design for bedsheets that begin cooling once the temperature in the room reaches a certain degrees. The premise of this product design project is to break down the logic behind a piece of usable technology that people can interact with in everyday life. This project uses problem solving through elements such as algorithmic thinking, pattern recognition and flowcharts.

Automation & the Human Laborer

In depth look at automation in the workforce and what it means for the human worker. This project examines the past, present, and future outlook of machines in the workplace while considering both the pros and cons of automation taking over jobs.

Rhetoric of Fox News

Technical and theoretical analysis of how the design, structure, and content of FoxNews.com affects the rhetoric the website is trying to emit. The purpose of this project was to show how design and structure of a website effects how the audience interacts with the site.

Ladybug House

Ladybug House is an organization looking for funding to set up a palliative care facility for sick children. Myself, along with a team of other Students, provided in depth technical reports on ways in which Ladybug House could improve their website with the goal of gaining more funding and support from other organizations and individuals. The volunteer work I provided included ways to make improvements such as accessibility to the site, search engine optimization, and design.
